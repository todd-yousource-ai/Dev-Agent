## What

Create the authoritative conflict resolution and decision precedence documentation for the Forge project, resolving all identified cross-TRD ambiguities across the 16 TRDs into a single source-of-truth hierarchy.

## Why

With 16 TRDs governing different subsystems, several areas of overlap and ambiguity exist:

- **Startup timeout values**: TRD-1 (macOS Application Shell) and TRD-12 (Health & Diagnostics) both specify timeout behavior with potentially conflicting defaults.
- **Code quality gates**: TRD-3 (Build Pipeline) and TRD-14 (Code Quality) both define quality gate thresholds and enforcement points.
- **Configuration key naming**: Multiple TRDs define configuration keys without a unified naming convention.
